When you start the game, you'll initially have a Reyna or Jett selection screen. If you choose Reyna, Q casts a blind spell, bots stop for 4 seconds, and bots you kill leave triangles. If you look at those triangles and press E, you'll regenerate your health. But if you choose Jett, Q casts a smoke spell, bots stop for 3 seconds, and E casts a dash to the area you're looking at. Weapons are 1 AKM and 2 knives. The AKM can be reloaded with R. This is related to AIM, but again, it's under development!
